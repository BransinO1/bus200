About Noveltea Books

At Noveltea Books, we blend the love for reading with the comfort of a warm cup of tea. Whether you're looking for your next page-turner or just a cozy spot to unwind, our bookstore offers a welcoming atmosphere for book lovers of all kinds.

Located in the heart of Rexburg, Idaho, we offer a wide selection of books, from the latest bestsellers to timeless classics. Our cafe serves a variety of teas, coffees, and light snacks to enjoy while you browse or read in our comfortable seating areas.
