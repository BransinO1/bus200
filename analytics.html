Personal Insight on Bookstores

Most of the bookstores that have emerged from research have products related to the owner's personal interests. Bookstores are unique and diverse, but common items like books are found across all of them. Including something personal, like a hobby or interest, could attract more customers. For example, it would be great if we included something, like a nail salon, to make it personal. It may be a little challenging to start, but it would be a unique and attention-grabbing feature!
